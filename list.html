Ordered and Unordered Lists

An unordered list uses ol and li tags. The ul tag declares the list type, and the li tags are the items.

List of People Who Have Forgotten To Not Be Samir

Descriptive lists use dl, dt, and dd. DL tag stands for descriptive list. Dt for descriptive topic. DD for descriptive data.
