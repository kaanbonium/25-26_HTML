Finger Tricks

  • Use your index finger to flick the top layer.
  • Push the bottom with your thumb instead of rotating the cube.
  • Keep moves small and controlled for faster speed.
  • Use thumb for all F moves.
  • Use ring finger for regular M' moves, middle finger for M moves, and roll your ring + middle finger for M2 moves.
  • Always use wrist for L or R moves.
  • Keep rotations fast, move your fingers before making a cube rotation.
  • When learning algs online, watch their fingers and copy for the most optimal finger tricks
